# Leet Speak
* **Description**: To explain leet speak, uses of leet speak and disadvantages of leet speak.

* **Leet Speak**: Leet Speak is a secret language developed by hackers in the 1980s to communicate over the Internet while bypassing Internet filters regarding dangerous hacking technologies. It is also known as "1337" or "leet".

* **Uses**: Leet Speak is used by hackers to communicate over the Internet, bypassing Internet filters, regarding dangerous hacking technologies. Additionally it is also used to generate difficult password combinations. The program leet-translate is used for this. But today it is more commonly used for obfuscation (hiding meaning from automated bots), gaming culture, and creative password masking.

* **Disadvantages**: Leet speak passwords will not overcome dictionary attacks. Additionally  can only write it can't tell it. The biggest drawback of Leet Speak is that a single letter has multiple symbols.
